Friday 1940
Went to see Jean. She looks quite well but very yellow. Saw Grandma She was sitting up Went to skating club. MISSED FIRST FRIDAY

Tuesday 1943
Grandpa Aunt Alice & Nancy came up Went over to Roxy's with Audrey Keyt. Went skating Learned Fox Trot and lady'o 14 step. Gordon Jennens came in for a while

Wednesday 1944
Mr & Mrs B were here for lunch. Went to Roxy's with Ber Woods after echo of Planning jam session for Sat night. Mom was in Van with Helen for supper Had long chat on phone with Ber again tonight Val also Audrey Keyt came in a minute.
