Wednesday 1940
Learned Credo of Mass of St Michael at singing. Went to music lesson got new piece called "Country Dance". Nicest I've ever had. Attended Miss Gilley's studio club. Joe's president.

Sunday 1943
Didn't go to Mass but stayed in bed all day with cold. Heard that it's 4 below zero out to-day. Wrote 16 page letter to Sr. M Loretto and started one to Sr. Fernando. Started designing houses in sketches.

Monday 1944
Stayed in bed all day with cold. Slept from 9-12 this AM. Wrote Sr Fernando and Sr M. Loretto both long letters Jo went with Bill Fox to see "The Corn Is Green" with Ethel Barrymore The Alan Lewis' came tos upper. They stayed here for quite awhile.
