Friday 1940
Everything froze over last night or silver that as they call it Mr Palson came in to try piano while gerry and Dad went to skating. Dad fell twice and has very sore hip.

Tuesday 1943
Grandpa and Aunt Alice came up. Showed us darling photo of Paula and little Jo. Bob phoned to say a last goodbye at noon. Went skating. Had super fun dancing with Pat Nevard. Mr. Leiois drove us home.

Wednesday 1944
Practised till supper Gave super arragement of When Day So Done. Started homework after supper and Fran phoned so I went over and did homework.
