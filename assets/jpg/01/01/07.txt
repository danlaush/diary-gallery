Sunday 1940
Rode on bike with Jenny Law nearly all morning. Went to skating club. Murray Brown was an awful show and so was Irene Strong. Gram coming home tomorrow.

Thursday 1943
Started back to school after holidays. Went over to Peterson's for awhile. Took Steve to the store. Went to RCHJA meeting at Molly McPherson's home. It's getting terribly cliquey! Talked with jean Ford. Found out Jack Tartman has to go to hospital for 6 months.

Friday 1944
Went to see "This Is The Army" all by myself Skipped last period Phoned Fran tonight Sammy and a super young Lt Bruce Gordon came for dinner She drove them home to Van and picked up Dad We sang and played till midnight I've got a terrific headache Missed basketball game.
