Friday 1940
Went to skating club but only for an hour as hockey match on. We were allowed in free so stayed. Had swell fun and learned lots of new jokes

Tuesday 1943
Had eliminations for Oratorical contest. Fran's super! Went skating. Had long talk with Deley. Wrote to Marie at noon hour. Started to snow again to-day.

Wednesday 1044
Walked up with Tommy Smith. He came in to practise singing with microphone. He's got Senatia down pat. Wnet over to Bernice's awhile. She'll be at school again on Monday Took lunch today. Mom told me we can have another party. made up a list of kids Phoned Ben.
