Wednesday 1940
Went to music lesson. Bobby Conn worse than ever. Went to Miss Gilley's Studio Club. All kids played pieces and we sang.

Saturday 1942
Jack hardly sober after rip roaring party last night Hope Gordon phones. Met Virginia Sullivan & Paul her friend she's ardling and Irish. Went to show to see Charles Boyer in Hold Back the Dawn at the University Theatre. It was super! Gee it's swell to be in Seattle. I can't believe I'm here.

Sunday 1943
Stayed in bed till about 2PM. Dad brought two Air Force fellas in for a drink. Boy did I scram in a hurry. Helen Shaun & Molly came over for dinner. Feel awfully tired because I hardly slept last night. Played violin cello & piano music all evening with Geri & Jo for a while then Dad & Mom. Gordon J came in for awhile later tonight

Monday 1944
Tonight Grandma helped me with Biology from 7:15-10:15 Had fun walking up after school with Sue Marilyn Glenn & Pete. Pead book after school 'Gine Them Their Dreams" Delcie phoned to say she & Bill broke up Saturday night.
