Thursday 1940
Went to Jean Douglas' house out at penn. Had swell fun RCHJA advisors, Mrs. Horsfold was there Helped Mom bake cake and make supper sifted flour 8 times.

Monday 1943
Cold not so good but went to school anyway. School closed for the afternoon so all the kids could go on a skating party to Burnaby Lake which is frozen solid. Started to snow the aft. and hasn't stopped yet. Stayed home and helped Mom serve at a meeting of the Altar society. Got letter from Mark. Can't remember if he's tall or short sailor at Doreas'.

Tuesday 1944
Gumpy & A Alice came up. Mr Male came to say good-bye. He's going overseas in the army on Thursday. Was up doing Algebra with Dad till nearly eleven.
