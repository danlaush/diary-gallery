Thursday 1940
Went down town after school and bought swell new suitcase. Practised three-part songs for concert. Varnished suitcase to make it water-proof.

Monday 1943
Missed school this afternoon and went to Dr Leo's again. Was early so window shopped at the Bay and Spencer. Was supposed to go to Mr. Wallace's for party but it was called off. Did Latin all evening. I'm going nuts!

Tuesday 1944
Made lunch but forgot it so had to come home. Got dressed up after school and Mom had Gumpy & A. Alice Mr & mrs Knight, Mr & Mrs Lavally Mr & Mrs Miller & Frank Knight for a huge turkey dinner. Jo Geri and I had to sing and play all evening. It was Frank's birthday so we had a huge cake for him.
