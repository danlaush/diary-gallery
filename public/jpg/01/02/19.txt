Monday 1940
Stayed in bed until 9:30 & had nap in afternoon from 1 to 4 PM. Thought of kids working hard ins chool. Saw Fritz Krusler. He was marvellous! Went to Omalley's for milkshake after.

Friday 1943
Went to Dr. Lea's this aft. Looked in Vurkes and MV Co for present for Fran but saw nothing I liked. Came to bed right after supper. Am getting a cold. Wrote to Mary and started note to Bob. I feel awful.

Saturday 1944
Waxed fall and den floors. Helped Mom this aft. Tonight Bev & Bob Trudie & Fred Peggy & Don Rosy & Ron Sue & Pete Vale & Don Jane & Cotty Edne & Stan Jean R & Billy L Jean C & Bill H Marta & Gordon Theila & Tut Mary & Glenn Marilyn & Don McR. Shirley & Art Sick S,Donny McD & me, Gordy C & Geri, Delc & Bill Marigrace & Ton, Mary C & Bill, Peggy T & Norm H, Owen Hickhem, Gerry E & Tommy, Doreen & Gary, Myrtle C & Jack G, Sal & Ted (for a while) came and daved in stocking feet to polish the floor. Jane & Cotty boke Mome's antique chair. She wasn't too mad Donny told me he couldnt' go to the Sweater Hop on Friday - nuts!
