Saturday 1940
Went to Choir. Fran and mary Turnbull were there. Swept and cleaned basement with Dad. Jo going to Spinster's Ball with Don Fraser.

Wednesday 1943
Miss school this afternoon and went to Vancouver to Dr Lea's. Stalled for awhile at Holy Rosary Cathedral by saying the Stations then had my through blessed Rode home on bus with Mr Leland. Went to music lesson He gave me all kinds of super music for violin cello and piano.

Thursday 1944
Went to RoHoHo meeting at Rachel Calders house. Deley & Shiela & Gaube were initiated We made them go up to strage houses and advertise. It was really a panic. Got kind of mad at Rory & Peggy because Don & Ron called for them when the meeting was barely over.
