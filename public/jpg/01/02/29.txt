Thursday 1940
Went to Mrs. J. R. Gilley's for fitting of costume for Rotary Ice Carnival. Costumes darling. Went to meeting of RCHJA at Marion Gunn's darling little new house.

Tuesday 1944
Leap Year Day. Teased Bill Hughes. Gee he's funny! He got out early to go to BC Products display at the Legion. All Tech kids there. Marilyn & I left early & went to Langley's via BC Electric Walked up with Glenn Gerry D. Bill C. George R. I laughed myself sick Tom McArthur & Tom Smith were here till supper practising for Boys [Yi Y Lil] tomorrow. Fooled all evening Went to Mass this AM.
