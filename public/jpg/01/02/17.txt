Saturday 1940
Went down down [town?] and saw The Fighting 69th with Jimmy Cagney and Pat O'Brien. June came over with little Joe who has grown an awful lot.

Wednesday 1943
Didn't go to music lesson because of too much homework. Studied English all evening. Gram still in bed with Flu but is much better to-day.

Thursday 1944 
Went to RoCoHo meeting at Marilyn Gilley's house. Our tea is shaping up nicely now. After the meeting Geri & I went down to Dr Pentlands and stayed with [name] until 1AM. Wrote Butch a long time. Gosh I'm tired!
